Suggested

  • 8 MB or more of RAM.
  • Microprocessor equal to or later than Intel Core i3.
  • Disk size is not relevant, although it is suggested to have 250 MB free.
  • MS Windows operating system v ≥ 10, Linux with kernel v ≥ 4, macOS v ≥10.
  • Browsers: Firefox v ≥ 50, Chrome v ≥ 51 (recommended), Opera v ≥ 40.
