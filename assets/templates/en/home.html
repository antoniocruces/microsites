What is Pathwise

Pathwise is a software development carried out by iArtHis_LAB Research Group, of the Department od Art History of the University of Málaga (Spain), as part of the projects Exhibitium FBBVA 2014 and ArtCatalogs (HAR2014-51915-P) to make available to researchers interested in the application of the digital paradigm to the study of the Humanities (Art History in particular) a versatile tool allowing quantitative analysis of qualitative information of the discipline.

ExpoFinder iArtHis_LAB DHA UMA

Pathwise use files in SQLite v3 format, generated by ExpoFinder. To obtain them you will need access credentials. Feel free to request them contacting development team.

How does it work

Pathwise is an application, hosted in an Internet server, deploying its power only by means of browser resources. It is entirely built using HTML as a pages description language, CSS as a style interpreter and Javascript (ES6) to execute the code.

Even though Pathwise is widely supported by most browsers, it is convenient to use the most up-to-date versions.

See the docs or contact development team if you want to know more details and in depth the possibilities and technology of Pathwise. You can also know its code and the different versions in the official repository.
